Answers To Your Questions

Q. Where in the world is the Cook Islands? - A. The 15 volcanic islands of the Cook Islands are scattered over 770,000 square miles of the South Pacific,and is located east of American Samoa and to the west of French Polynesia.

Q. What is the weather like? - A. The weather in the Cook Islands can be unpredictable. It will be sunny in the morning but come the afternoon, you have heavy rain.

Q What are the best times of the year to travel? - The best time to travel depends on what you plan to do. If you want to see the culture, a good time to come is late July and early August.

Q. Is it a safe place to visit? - The Cook Islands is one of the safest places to visit. It does not mean that we are crime free but you can go on the road at night and not worry about anything happening to you.

Q. What should they bring when visiting? - The temperature throughout the varies from 18 to 35 degrees Celsius in the southern group islands and higher in the northern group. If you are travelling to the Cook Islands, you do not need to bring so many warm clothes.

Q. Are there any major events that happen on the island, annually, and what time of the year does that happen? - There are numerous events that happen annually, come visit and find out.
